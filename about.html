Our core values

Taiga Shipping & Logistics Company Ltd was established in 1996 with its headquarters located in Milan, Italy. We have over 300 branches currently, all over the world.


We take the promises you make to your customers very seriously. To fulfil these promises, we commit to our fundamental beliefs: Entrepreneurship, Expertise, Engagement and Excellence. These core values reflect the heritage of our company, our culture, where we are today and our ambitions for the future.

Our Mission & Vision

The Company has presence in over 2400 major air and sea ports worldwide with our partnership with WCA (World Cargo Alliance) and JC Trans.

Our misson is to partner with clients & other stakeholders to deliver end-to-end supply chain, best practices and solutions in a timely and secure manner at competitive rates.


Our ultimate vision is to be a household name and a leading logistics provider in the entire world.

Over the years the Company has expanded its services from customs brokerage to Freight forwarding which includes warehousing(Bonded and Non Bonded), Diplomatic movements, Removal services (Household and corporate),Courier Services, Hauling, Project Cargo Movement, Ground Handling, Heavy duty Movement, Cross Boarder Transportation, Aviation Cargo Experts


We are committed to maximizing productivity and efficiency by delivering uniquely designed Shipping and Logistics solutions to both corporate and individual clients.

Taiga Shipping Company Limited, with long standing experience in its field of operations, is ever ready to take over any job when called upon. The company has grown by providing high quality logistical services to meet the needs of operators and producers. It is in the forefront of quality development in the region, working to the world-class standards with a continual program of improvement.
